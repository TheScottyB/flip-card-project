Who Qualifies?

Available nationwide (except New York)
Purchase only (no refinances)
No income limits
Minimum 600 FICO score
Loan-to-Value (LTV) 90% - 96.5%
Eligible properties:
- Primary residences (1-2 units)
- Single-family homes, PUDs, townhomes, condos, attached/detached homes, modular & manufactured homes
Non-occupant borrowers allowed (family members or relatives only)
